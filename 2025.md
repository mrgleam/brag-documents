* Led the development of a banking operations platform supporting branch-level services—an area with high operational criticality but low ownership. The project operated under high uncertainty with continuously evolving requirements and a fixed deadline.

* **Impact**: Enabled critical branch operations with a centralized platform. Increased stakeholder confidence through consistent delivery and reduced project risk.
* **Strengths Demonstrated**: Effective execution in high-uncertainty environments, strong ownership of ambiguous problem spaces, and ability to align business expectations with legacy system realities.
